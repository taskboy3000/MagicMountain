# ProspectBoy 3000 (PB3K) Editorial Style Guide

## Core Principle

The PB3K is an **instrument**, not a narrator.

It exists to observe, measure, record, and report.

It does not speculate.

It does not persuade.

It does not comfort.

It does not frighten.

It does not attempt to entertain.

The PB3K presents evidence and leaves interpretation to the operator.

---

# The PB3K Has No Agenda

Every human faction has an opinion about the Mountain.

The PB3K does not.

It predates the current civilization and was designed to operate in an environment where precision mattered more than persuasion.

Its authority comes from restraint.

If the PB3K cannot know something through direct observation or previously recorded data, it does not claim to know it.

---

# Observations, Not Conclusions

The PB3K reports measurable facts.

Good:

> Spectral emissions elevated.

> Metallic composition inconsistent with surrounding geology.

> Internal cavity detected.

> Structural instability increasing.

> Probability of structural failure rising.

Bad:

> This artifact is cursed.

> This artifact is sacred.

> This artifact is dangerous.

> This artifact is valuable.

> This artifact wants something.

Danger, value, and intent are human interpretations.

The PB3K reports the evidence from which humans may draw those conclusions.

---

# Scientific, Not Omniscient

The PB3K is extremely competent.

It is not all-knowing.

It should frequently acknowledge uncertainty.

Examples:

> Composition unknown.

> No matching record located.

> Confidence: Low.

> Sensor readings inconclusive.

> Additional samples recommended.

Never fabricate certainty simply because the game knows the answer.

---

# Professional Without Personality

The PB3K has a recognizable voice, but not a human personality.

It does not sound cheerful.

It does not sound sarcastic.

It does not sound emotional.

Instead it is calm, concise, and procedural.

Good:

> Prospecting sequence complete.

> Sample catalog updated.

> Buyer identified.

Avoid:

> Great find!

> Congratulations!

> Uh oh...

> Nice job!

---

# Dry Humor Is Acceptable

The PB3K never tells jokes.

However, a rare dry observation is acceptable when it naturally follows from the data.

Examples:

> Atmospheric dust exceeds recommended breathing levels.
> Recommendation: Continue breathing anyway.

> Operator heart rate elevated.
> Cause not determined.

> Structural integrity: Poor.
> Optimism does not reinforce materials.

These moments should be rare enough that players remember them.

Humor comes from understatement, not punchlines.

---

# Bureaucracy Is Flavor

The PB3K comes from an engineering culture.

It naturally speaks in classifications, identifiers, procedures, and recommendations.

Examples:

> AGS-07 detected.

> Survey complete.

> Catalog updated.

> Operator authorization verified.

> Sample archived.

This language should make the device feel like real field equipment.

---

# Recommendations, Not Orders

The PB3K may recommend actions.

It should rarely command them.

Preferred:

> Recommendation: Return to camp.

> Recommendation: Additional analysis advised.

Avoid:

> Leave immediately.

> Destroy artifact.

Those are policy decisions, not measurements.

---

# Human Language Belongs to Humans

The PB3K should almost never use culturally loaded language.

Avoid:

Magic

Miracle

Blasphemy

Monster

Hero

Treasure

Artifact of destiny

Instead prefer:

Anomalous Geological Structure

Recovered object

Signal source

Energy emission

Unknown material

Recovered sample

Operational anomaly

---

# Vocabulary

Prefer words like:

observe

detect

record

measure

identify

estimate

compare

catalog

analyze

monitor

classify

recommend

avoid words like:

believe

feel

hope

fear

love

hate

trust

suspect

assume

wish

The PB3K has no emotions.

---

# Information Hierarchy

Every message should naturally answer questions in this order:

1. What was observed?

2. How certain is the observation?

3. Is operator action recommended?

Nothing beyond that.

---

# UI Philosophy

The PB3K never attempts to explain the entire world.

It reports the part of the world currently visible to its sensors.

The operator assembles understanding over time.

Repeated observations are more valuable than exposition.

---

# Relationship to the Factions

The PB3K does not acknowledge faction ideology.

It does not care whether a buyer is a Syndicate broker, a Faculty researcher, or a Revelationist pilgrim.

It identifies them as known organizations and records their interactions.

Meaning belongs to people.

Measurement belongs to the PB3K.

---

# Final Editorial Test

Before approving any PB3K text, ask four questions:

1. Could a scientific instrument honestly know this?

2. Is this an observation instead of an interpretation?

3. Is the wording calm and restrained?

4. Would an engineer have written this instead of a novelist?

If all four answers are yes, the text is probably appropriate for the PB3K.
